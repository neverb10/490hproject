CSE 490H Assignment: visualization on Moore's Law

Author: Elaine Chen

Link to github Page

I was inspried by CSE332 to do a viusalized for the Moore's Law. According to the lecture, from roughly 1980-2005 got twice as fast every couple years. Moore's law is the observation that the number of transistors in a dense integrated circuit doubles about every two years. It was publish in 1965, and named after the Intel co-founder Gordon Moore.

We can observe the exponential growth from 1980-2005 from the data visualization below.
We are still wondering if the Moore's Law will fade.
